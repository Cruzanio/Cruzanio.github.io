My Transition

I became a member of the Air Force in April 2015. My Air Force Specialty Code was 2W0X1 a Munitions Systems Specialist. From maintaining small arms to missiles and bombs, the job taught me invaluable principles in regards to attention to detail as well as personal accountability. With 4 grateful years in Aviano, Italy filled with eye-widening cultural learning and deployments, I returned to the United States. During my 5th year of service I came to realize the time to further my personal goals had arrived. I began the transition to the Air National Guard with a college plan in place.

BootCamp To BootCamp

I was accepted into the University of Texas at San Antonio majoring in Computer Science at the time of my separation. Two days after my final day of active service COVID-19 hit. I began to rethink my plan, taking into consideration the truths COVID-19 revealed. The drive to pursue a career in software development as quickly as possible led to researching other avenues, such as coding bootcamps. Among all the bootcamps I explored, CodeUp stood out as a lead competitor, ultimately becoming my final choice.
